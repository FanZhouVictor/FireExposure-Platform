Location & Time Record

Build a continuous time-location timeline

  • 📍 GPS-based location capture with timestamp
  • ⚙️ Duty modes: always-on, on-shift, on-incident, manual
  • 🎯 Geofence triggers for stations & scenes
  • 📶 Offline buffering & delayed upload
  • 🧩 Timeline reconstruction → segments & stays
03
2

Fire Event Linkage

Connect location timeline to fire activities

  • 📝 Create fire event records with time windows
  • 🔗 Link user timeline segments to events
  • 🏷️ Event types: structural, wildland, training, overhaul, rehab
  • ✏️ Manual correction for weak GPS (indoor work)
04
3

Participation & Exposure

Record what firefighters did & conditions faced

  • 👤 Roles: inside, outside, vehicle, rehab, command
  • 🔧 Tasks: attack, search, ventilation, overhaul, salvage
  • 🛡️ PPE/SCBA use per individual
  • 💨 Exposure notes: smoke, soot, heat, decon
  • 🎤 Fast input: voice workflow, document scan, image upload
05
4

PPE Lifecycle

Connect exposures to gear used & condition

  • 📦 PPE inventory per user
  • 📅 Lifecycle: manufacture, in-service, usage hours
  • 🧹 Cleaning & decontamination history
  • 🔨 Damage & repair log with photos
  • ⏰ Inspection & cleaning reminders
06
5

Passive Sampling & Lab

Record chemical measurements & link to timelines

  • 🏷️ Sampler assignment tracking (ID, event, times)
  • 📊 Lab results ingest via CSV
  • 🔗 Link results to events & locations
  • 📈 Exposure summaries by time & event type
⚠️ No medical risk prediction in v1 — research-grade exports only
